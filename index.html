Warrish Card Game

There are two players in this game and a deck of 52 cards. The deck is dealt evenly between the two players at random. When the game is played, each player takes turns laying a card down in the middle until a match is played. At this point the player who plays the matching card takes the matching cards and all the other cards in the middle and puts them into a separate pile (the "points" pile). The game is played until one player runs out of cards and at that point the player whose "point" pile has more cards wins!
